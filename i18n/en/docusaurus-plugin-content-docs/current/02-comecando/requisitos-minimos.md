# Minimum requirements

Before starting the program activities, it is important to check whether you meet the minimum conditions to follow the training and complete the practical steps comfortably.

This page presents a basic list of technical and operational requirements. Depending on the class, the tool being used, and the way the activities are conducted, there may be additional guidance.

## Basic requirements

To participate properly, you must have:

- 💻 a computer with internet access, a *Windows* operating system, and permission to install programs.

## When to ask for help

Seek support from the training team if:

- a website does not open;
- the main tool does not load;
- there is an access or permission error;
- you do not know which environment you should use;
- your computer is too limited to follow the steps.
